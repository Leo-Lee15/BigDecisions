<p>Young Australians are often faced with the big decision whether to stay in the city they were raised in, or move interstate for employment opportunities or to be with a loved one. It's a big life decision. But how do you decide? Should I stay or should I go?
Adelaide loses lots of young graduates. They may earn more interstate but what about housing affordability or lifestyle?
Our website pulls together relevant data points to help individuals reach a meaningful decision.</p>
